# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This is my first data analysis project where I analyzed a coffee shop dataset to extract insights and help improve business performance.

## ❓ Business Questions
- What’s the total number of orders?
- What’s the total sales amount?
- Which drink is the most/least popular?
- What are the busiest days and months?
- What is the most used payment method?

## 📊 Key Insights
- Total Orders: 1,313  
- Total Sales: 42,627.58  
- Most Popular Drink: Americano with Milk  
- Least Popular Drink: Cocoa  
- Busiest Day: Tuesday  
- Busiest Time: Morning  

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on morning peak hours  
- Promote low-selling drinks  
- Apply successful strategies from May  
- Offer deals on Sundays  
- Improve digital payment experience

- Excel / Power BI
